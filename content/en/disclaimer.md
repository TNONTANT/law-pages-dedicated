## Not Medical Advice

SnapCal is a personal nutrition tracking tool. It is **not** a medical service. All analyses and recommendations displayed in SnapCal do not constitute diagnosis, treatment, prevention, or management of any disease or medical condition.

---

## AI Accuracy

- SnapCal uses AI to analyse food photos. All nutrient values, calories, and portion estimates are **approximations**, not precise measurements.
- Estimated accuracy: **70–80%** for common foods — may be lower for complex Thai dishes with hidden ingredients (oil, sauces, sugar used in cooking) or photos taken in poor lighting.
- TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula — a statistical average, not a measured value specific to you.
- Do not use SnapCal figures to make medical decisions.

---

## Not a Medical Device

SnapCal is not certified or registered as a medical device under the Thai Medical Device Act B.E. 2562. Information displayed cannot replace medical measurements.

---

## Consult a Doctor Before Use If You

- Have diabetes, heart disease, hypertension, or kidney disease
- Have a history of or are being treated for an eating disorder (e.g., anorexia, bulimia)
- Are pregnant or breastfeeding
- Are under 18 years of age
- Are taking medications that interact with diet or metabolism
- Have a medical condition requiring strict dietary management as prescribed by a doctor
